Tips for Protecting Against Air Pollution

Avoid outdoor activities during times of high pollution, especially near busy roads or industrial areas.

Use public transportation, carpool, or walk whenever possible to reduce vehicle emissions.

Keep windows and doors closed during periods of poor air quality, and use air purifiers indoors if available.

Check air quality forecasts regularly and plan outdoor activities accordingly.

Avoid burning wood, trash, or other materials outdoors, as this can contribute to air pollution.

Quit smoking and avoid exposure to secondhand smoke to protect respiratory health.

Stay informed about local air quality alerts and follow recommendations from health authorities.

Support policies and initiatives aimed at reducing air pollution and promoting clean energy sources.
